## 📌 Project Overview

An interactive sales performance dashboard built in **Power BI** using the popular Superstore dataset. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales, profit, and order trends across categories, sub-categories, and years — enabling data-driven business decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Total Sales | $2.30M |
| Total Profit | $286.40K |
| Total Orders | 5K |
| Total Customers | 793 |
| Total Quantity | 38K |

### 📈 Highlights:
- **Technology** leads in both sales ($836K) and profit ($145K)
- **Furniture** has high sales but the lowest profit margin (only $18K) — indicating cost issues
- **Copiers** and **Phones** are the top-performing sub-categories by profit
- **Tables** and **Bookcases** are loss-making sub-categories
- Profit grew **+32.74%** in 2018 and continued growing YoY after an initial drop

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Review Furniture pricing** — high revenue but very low profit margin
2. **Invest more in Technology** — strongest profit driver
3. **Consider discontinuing Tables** — consistently generating losses
4. **Focus on Copiers & Phones** — highest return on sales
